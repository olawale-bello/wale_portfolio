Chesterfield

Scroll Down

Services

Print, Digital

Client

Bombay Group

Year

2025

During my time at Yolo, I led creative production for Chesterfield Poker Club, a premium casino club in the heart of Tallinn. My role involved developing a wide range of visual and motion assets from video ads and social media content to web banners and large-scale OOH campaigns.

The challenge was to capture the energy and exclusivity of the casino experience while maintaining sharp and adaptable communication across multiple platforms. Each campaign had to strike a balance between sophistication and excitement, ensuring the brand stood out both digitally and in the physical city environment.

Through bold visuals, dynamic motion graphics, and strategic messaging, I helped strengthen Chesterfield’s presence as a vibrant entertainment destination. The consistent creative output not only drove visibility but also reinforced the club’s identity as a premium yet approachable space for gaming and social experiences.
